I'm Loïs Postula, a platform engineer based in Belgium.

I work on the technical foundations behind reliable software delivery:
self-hosted and cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, build systems, observability,
security, private deployments, and the operational work required to keep
software useful after it has been deployed.

At Foresight Spatial Labs, I work across the platform layer that supports
engineering and customer deployments: infrastructure, automation, monitoring,
delivery foundations, and security-related operational controls.

My background started in product engineering and backend work before moving
toward cloud and platform engineering. I still enjoy being close to the
machinery: building systems, debugging weird failures, and making complex
things repeatable.

I write occasionally about infrastructure-as-code, automation, build systems,
and the practical trade-offs behind running software in the real world.
